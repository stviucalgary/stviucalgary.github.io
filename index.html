How it works

The walls of the office space are not your average drywall. In fact, they are not even walls at all! The sides of our offices are made of tiny little robots called connector bots that can band together to create unique structures!

Using our augmented reality app, you can customize the space to your liking. Either pick a pre-built template or allow your employees personalize the area themselves and watch the connector bots do their magic!
